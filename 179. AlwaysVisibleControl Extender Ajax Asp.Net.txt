It is a simple Extender Control in Ajax Toolkit That allow us to pin control to the page so that they appear to float with page when it is scrolled or resized

-----------------

Important Properties of AlwaysVisibleControl Extender
------------------------------------------------------

 TargetControlID="Image1" 

 VerticalSide="Bottom" 

 HorizontalSide="Right" 

 UseAnimation="true"

-------------------------------------------------------
