What is ListSearchExtender?

Ans: It Provides the ability to search items in Listbox and DropDownlist Control by Typing. It performs searching on the basis of typed input.

---------

Important Properties of ListSearchExtender :

TargetControlID="DropDownList1"

 PromptText="Type ID to Search" 

PromptPosition="Top" 

QueryPattern="StartsWith" 

IsSorted="true"
